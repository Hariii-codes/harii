My Dearest Shakes,

As I sit down to write this letter, my heart is overflowing with emotions that words can hardly capture. Yet, I’ll try my best to pour my feelings onto this page, hoping that every word conveys just how much you mean to me....

YFrom the moment you came into my life, everything changed. You brought with you a light that I didn’t even know I was missing. Your smile, your laughter, your kindness—they’ve become the most beautiful parts of my world. You have this incredible way of making even the simplest moments feel magical, and I can’t help but fall for you more and more every single day..

Shakes, you are the most amazing person I’ve ever known. Your strength inspires me, your warmth comforts me, and your love completes me. You’ve shown me what it means to truly care for someone, to be selfless, and to love unconditionally. With you, I feel like I’ve found my home—a place where I belong, where I’m understood, and where I’m loved for exactly who I am.

You’ve taught me so much about love, Shakes. Love isn’t just about grand gestures or perfect moments; it’s about the little things—the quiet mornings, the shared smiles, the way you hold my hand when we walk together. It’s about being there for each other, through the highs and the lows, and knowing that no matter what, we’ll always have each other’s backs.

On this Valentine’s Day, I want to ask you something that I’ve been dreaming about for so long. Will you be my Valentine, not just today, but every day? Will you let me love you, support you, and stand by your side through all of life’s adventures? Because there’s no one else I’d rather share this journey with than you. Thank you for being you, Shakes. Thank you for loving me, for believing in me, and for making my life so much brighter. You are my greatest blessing, and I’ll spend the rest of my life trying to make you as happy as you’ve made me.

With all my love, now and forever,.

Yours Hariii
